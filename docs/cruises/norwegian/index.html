Norwegian Cruise Line - Freestyle Cruising Revolution

Norwegian Cruise Line revolutionized cruising with Freestyle Cruising, offering the flexibility to dine, dress, and play however you want. With no fixed dining times, dress codes, or traditional cruise formality, Norwegian delivers the ultimate freedom at sea. With 18 innovative ships sailing to 14 destinations worldwide, Norwegian offers the perfect cruise vacation for every traveler.

What Makes Norwegian Different?

Norwegian revolutionized the cruise industry with Freestyle Cruising, eliminating the rigid schedules and formal requirements of traditional cruising. Choose from up to 25 dining venues, dress however you feel comfortable, and enjoy Broadway-caliber entertainment on your schedule.
