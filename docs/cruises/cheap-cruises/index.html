How to Get the Cheapest Cruise Deals

📅

Best Times to Book

  • •Wave Season (Jan-Mar) for best promotions
  • •Last minute (30-60 days out) for deep discounts
  • •Shoulder season (Apr-May, Sep-Nov)
  • •Repositioning cruises (one-way routes)
🛏️

Cabin Selection Tips

  • •Interior cabins save 40-50% vs. balconies
  • •Guarantee cabins for lowest prices
  • •Lower decks cost less than upper decks
  • •Quad occupancy splits costs for families
🚢

Cruise Line Savings

  • •Older ships offer same fun at lower prices
  • •Shorter cruises (3-5 days) cost less
  • •Drive-to ports save on airfare
  • •Look for "Kids Sail Free" promotions
💳

Onboard Savings

  • •Book packages pre-cruise for discounts
  • •Bring your own wine (where allowed)
  • •Explore ports on your own vs. ship tours
  • •Take advantage of free dining options
👨‍👩‍👧‍👦
