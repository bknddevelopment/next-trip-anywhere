Why Carnival is America's Favorite Cruise Line

Carnival Cruise Line has earned its reputation as America's Cruise Line by offering affordable, fun-filled vacations that appeal to all ages. With more ships departing from US homeports than any other cruise line, Carnival makes it easy to cruise without expensive flights. Their "Choose Fun" philosophy means non-stop entertainment, delicious dining that's mostly included, and innovative attractions you won't find anywhere else.

Carnival's Fleet Innovation

  • Excel Class: The newest and largest ships featuring BOLT roller coaster, 6 themed zones, and over 20 dining venues on Mardi Gras, Carnival Celebration, and Carnival Jubilee
  • Vista Class: Featuring SkyRide suspended cycling, IMAX Theatre, and Havana-themed areas
  • Dream Class: Popular ships with WaterWorks, mini golf, and Serenity Adult Retreat
  • Conquest Class: Classic favorites perfect for families and first-time cruisers
