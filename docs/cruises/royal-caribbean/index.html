The Royal Caribbean Difference

Royal Caribbean International has revolutionized cruising with industry-first innovations that turn ordinary vacations into extraordinary adventures. From the record-breaking Icon of the Seas with its Category 6 water park to Perfect Day at CocoCay's thrilling attractions, Royal Caribbean delivers experiences you won't find with any other cruise line.

Fleet Innovation & Ship Classes

  • Icon Class: The newest and largest ships in the world, featuring 8 distinct neighborhoods and the first suspended infinity pool at sea
  • Oasis Class: Revolutionary mega-ships with Central Park, Boardwalk, and the AquaTheater
  • Quantum Class: High-tech vessels with North Star observation capsules, RipCord skydiving, and Two70 venues
  • Freedom Class: Family-friendly ships with FlowRider surf simulators and ice skating rinks
  • Voyager Class: The ships that started it all with rock climbing walls and Royal Promenades

Perfect Day at CocoCay - Exclusive Paradise

Royal Caribbean's massive transformation of their private island in the Bahamas has created the ultimate cruise destination. Perfect Day at CocoCay features Thrill Waterpark with 13 slides, the tallest waterslide in North America (Daredevil's Peak), the largest wave pool in the Caribbean, and the Up, Up and Away helium balloon offering 450-foot high panoramic views. Most Royal Caribbean Caribbean itineraries now include a stop at this award-winning destination.
